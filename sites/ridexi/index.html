About Us

RideXi is a company that lets you choose which car you want to ride with and who to support when travelling. By having the local car service companies at your fingertips you can choose who to travel with.

Becasue of these choices, your ride will arrive quicker and get to your destination quicker too. Plus you can even choose a car based on price, or ratings.

At the end, why take away that choice from the customer? We let you decide who you want to ride with!

Services

Just click and relax, your car is coming to get you. Whenever and where ever you need a ride, RideXi has a network to pick you up fast, and get you to your destination at an affordable price you will be happy to pay.

You will also support your local car service companies by utlilizing their friendly staff. No need to worry about carrying cash, just use your existing debit or credit card to get your next ride.

Your Local Car Service is probably just around the corner, just choose who, and they'll come get you.
